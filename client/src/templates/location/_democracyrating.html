• Level 1 = Freedoms of expression, assembly, media available ("human rights")
  • Level 2 = Rights to elect and to be elected available ("delegative rights")
  • Level 3 = Basic Participatory possibilities beyond elections as eg petition and agenda initiatives available ("participatory rights")
  • Level 4 = Direct (binding) Democratic processes available like initiative and referendum ("DD rights")
  • Level 5 = Citizen activation opportunities available linked to CA-APP (future feature)
